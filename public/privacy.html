Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Saint of the Day is operated by Michael Duggan, an individual developer based in the Netherlands. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — collected by Lemon Squeezy when you subscribe, used to verify your subscription status when you access the app.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We never see or store your card details.
  • License key — generated by Lemon Squeezy after purchase and used to authenticate your access to the app.

We do not collect your name, location, device identifiers, or any usage data about how you use the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify that you have an active subscription when you open the app.
  • To send you your license key and subscription receipts (via Lemon Squeezy).

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Lemon Squeezy processes payments and manages subscriptions on our behalf. They act as the merchant of record and handle all billing, VAT, and payment data. Their privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Vercel hosts the app. Standard server logs (IP address, request time) may be retained by Vercel for security purposes. Their privacy policy is at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Wikipedia provides saint portrait images displayed in the app. No personal data is sent to Wikipedia.

4. Data Retention

Your email address and subscription status are retained by Lemon Squeezy for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion by cancelling your subscription and contacting us at the address below.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a resident of the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at michaelduggan@pm.me and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date at the top.
